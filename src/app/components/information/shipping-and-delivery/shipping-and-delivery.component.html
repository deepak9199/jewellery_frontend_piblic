Shipping And Delivery

Shipping and Delivery

We ship to all locations within India.

Shipping Rates

  • Orders over ₹1000 - Free Shipping
  • Orders under ₹1000 - ₹50 Flat Rate Shipping

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on the shipping method selected at checkout.

  • Standard Shipping - 3 to 5 business days
  • Expedited Shipping - 2 to 3 business days
  • Express Shipping - 1 to 2 business days

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track your order on our website or on the carrier's website.

International Shipping

At this time, we do not offer international shipping.

Customer Support

If you have any questions about shipping and delivery, please contact our customer support team.
